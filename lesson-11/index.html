Weekend winter storms bring heavy snow accumulations to mountains, rain/snow to valley

by Sarah Jacobsen

A Winter Weather Advisory is in effect for portions of southeastern Idaho through 11am Saturday for up to 4" in accumulations and freezing rain. If you are traveling in southeastern Idaho west through the Snake River Plain, be ready for icy conditions on roads and make sure to take it slow.
