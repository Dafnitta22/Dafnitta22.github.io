Target Audience

Women and men who are between 18-70 years old. They have an active life by doing outdoor activities both for work and for fun. They are very responsible and organized in all their activities, also they are adventurous and love to travel around the world.


Persona:

Susie Boyer

  • Occupation: Flight Attendant
  • Income: $70,000
  • Age: 55 years old
  • Status: Married
  • Education: College Graduate
  • Children: 3 (Adults)
  • Devices: Phone, Tablet and Laptop.

Goals and Motivation: She is planning to keep working on this Job until her retirement. She needs to check the weather to pack the items that she will need for her trips. She works by the schedule that it could change any time during a year. She sets aside time to spend with her family and friends in her free days.

Walter Smith

  • Occupation: High School Coach
  • Income: $45,000
  • Age: 35 years old
  • Status: Single
  • Education: Bachelor Degree
  • Children: None
  • Devices: Phone and Tablet

Goals and Motivation: He is very organized with his career because of the teams that he is coaching. Moreover, he loves to take time to go to the mountains or navigate at the lake, so he takes time to do these activities with family or friends.


Scenarios:

  1. Do I have to carry a jacket and umbrella on my next trip? Susie has a trip to San Francisco where the weather is unstable, and she needs to pack everything in her little suitcase.
  2. Walter is thinking about to gather with his friends at the lake this weekend. What's the weather going to be this weekend at the lake?
  3. Every day, Walter has the same question while he is planning his daily activities. Where should we have our training today?
  4. Susie is going to the Airport, and it is a cloudy day. She has to check the weather because she will find traffic if it rains. Should I take this road today?
  5. Where should we celebrate Paul’s birthday? Susie is planning to celebrate her son’s birthday in her house. She has to check the weather if they celebrate inside the home or at the patio.
